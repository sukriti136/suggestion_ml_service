Everyday Checking fees and details


Monthly service fee

$10 each fee period

You can avoid the $10 monthly service fee with one of the following each fee period:

  • $500 minimum daily balance
  • $500 or more in total qualifying electronic deposits
  • The primary account owner is 17 - 24 years old
  • A linked Wells Fargo Campus ATM Card or Campus Debit Card (for college students)
  • A qualifying monthly non-civilian military direct deposit with the Wells Fargo Worldwide Military Banking program
